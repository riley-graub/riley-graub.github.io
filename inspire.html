My Personal Hero

My Little Brother

When I think of a hero, I think of someone who is brave, trustworthy, courageous, and valorus. Someone who is willing to do things that make others' lives better. A hero does things in an act of kindness or love, with an intent to do better. When I think of these heroic qualities, I instantly think of my brother, Kane.

Kane is my younger brother, whom I consider my personal hero. Ever since the moment he was born and I got to hold him for the first time, I knew this kid was going to make a difference in my life for the better. Not only has he lived up to that expectation, but he has exceeded what I thought he would ever be to me. With there being a seven year age difference, I thought I would be the hero that he would look up to. Over the last few years, I realized that I got that completley wrong. He is the hero that I look up to the most, and rely on when things get tough. He teachs me how to be a better person and how to push myself out of my comfort-zone. He guides me through hard times, helps me solve problems, teaches me new things that benefit my life, and brings attention to the things that I shouldn't be doing. All of these characteristics are what make him my personal hero and will forever keep him my personal hero.

My brother was one of the best people to ever be brought into my life. He has been by my side for 13 years now and I know there is so many more to come. No one can ever compare to the ways he has impacted my life and continues to. I wouldn't be anything near the woman I am today without him. Kane will forever be my little hero!
